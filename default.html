Hi, I'm Tudor and I like to express myself through interactive means of escapism.   Let's see if we can be best buds.

I have degrees in Computer Science, Industrial Design, and Game Technology. My approach is technical but interdisciplinary. I'm an aficionado of gameplay, simulations and data oriented programming. I also have fun researching PCG, VFX, and physics or mechanics using GPGPU.

Interactive software = science + craft. Over the years I picked my studies to learn to research the most tractable solutions by leveraging the technology in an artistically aware fashion.

Valve's Employee Handbook discusses the need for devs with a high breadth of skills as well as deeper expertise in certain fields, and that an interest in different parts of the spectrum brings innovation and achieves great, dare I say it, "synergy".
