Welcome

I'm Tudor and I enjoy developing interactive escapism.

I have degrees in Game Technology, Computer Science, and Industrial Design, a programmer with a feel for the creative.

I work with and research Gameplay, Procedural Content, Virtual Reality, Tools, VFX, and am a GPGPU enthusiast. Data Oriented Design vs OOP.

Let's make cool things together! I aim for leveraging emerging affordances, fun interaction, synergizing with the artistic perspective, and know about the Dunning–Kruger effect.

I agree with the Valve Handbook advice for devs to be interested in a few different parts of the spectrum. It helps bring about new tractable solutions, optimization, and good team mojo.
