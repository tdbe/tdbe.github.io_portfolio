"Holosuit" Game Concept
ITU Mini Solo Project
June 2013

|This project was part of a Concept Art and In-Game Graphics course; about designing a game with focus on environment, and illustrating how the mechanics and gameplay come across.


| The pitch: fantasy characters have Green Lantern style force-field suits, which take any shape the caracter imagines. Character class and suit visuals are a consequence of the character's personality and tastes.

Powersuit
Is Character
Persona
Suit is both
Shield and
Weapon

| The core mechanics: Suit morphs for attacks but its volume is limited. Player decides between defence and offence. Suit == shield && Suit == weapon.

Unmetered freedom to spam your most powerful attacks.
The penalty: exposed body is punishable / vulnerable, and suit can deplete / shrink.


| Can use entire suit for ranged attack.

Game concept: 2.5D beat-em-up platformer. With drop-in co-op and RPG elements of Monster Hunter + capcom D&D / Dragon's Crown. The openness and depth of the zones depends on the tech used for assets (sprites vs 3D) and procedural content.
