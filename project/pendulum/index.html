|A 3 week solo competence project for DADIU where I designed and implemented a game concept of my own.


What is the most interesting way to go about through a game environment? What are the character mechanics and the atmosphere? What is the series of interesting constraints to facilitate fun gameplay?


| The pitch: you're a pebble with a Gish-type blob counterweight / body. You control the shape of the tar to sling yourself, punch, drill, block, stick, jump or ricochet your way through the level. Taking damage == loosing tar == less powers.
