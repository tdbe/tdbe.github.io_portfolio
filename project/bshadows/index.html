"Broken Shadows" Alpha
ITU 3Mo 5Man Project
June 2013

|Second semester game development project.

A mini alpha version of a AAA concept: a shipwreck survivor's, narrative driven exploration, of a strange canyon scape.

The concept: Canyons with grappling hooks and climbing. Light sensitive monsters lurking in the cracks and shadows.
You are following the traces of another survivor and his injured freind.

Swing,
Climb and
Survive
Beams
of Light
Burn Swarms

My responsibilities:

-Grappling hook / swinging mechanics.
-Malevolent swarms (placeholder graphics). They are sun and torch sensitive.
-Procedural dynamic shadow-enemy spawner, and ray ( reverse-stealth) based light-damage.
-Pipeline programming, asset integration.
-HUD, pickups.
-Kismet materials.
-Some level, mechanic and game design, and art direction (yes, I had to 'help' with everything).
-User testing/evaluation.
