|This started as a short research project (2013) into using Compute Shaders to manipulate voxel grids.
It was integrated as a modelling and visualizing tool in my PCG Caves Thesis.


There are 643 entities with color and velocities. I also generate 6^2 triangle (cube, or quad) in a geometry pass for each particle (3.145.728 tris).
