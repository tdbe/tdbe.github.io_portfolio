Welcome.

I'm Tudor and I like programming interactive escapism.

With degrees in Computer Science, Industrial Design, and Game Technology, I'm a software dev who also manages to be creative and designerly.

I work with and research Gameplay, Data Oriented systems, Virtual Reality, Procedural Content Generation, Tools, VFX, and am a GPGPU enthusiast.

I aim to create the most tractable solutions, research ways of leveraging emerging affordances, and have an understanding of the artistic perspective.

I agree with the Valve handbook advice for devs to have hobbies in different parts of the spectrum. It helps bring about disruptive solutions, and good team mojo.
