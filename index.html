Hi, I'm Tudor and I like to express myself through means of escapism. Let's see if we can be best buds.

I am a developer with degrees in Computer Science, Industrial Design, Game Technology.
My approach is technical but interdisciplinary. I'm an aficionado of gameplay, simulations and data oriented programming. I like having fun with PCG, VFX and physics or mechanics using GPGPU.

Interactive software = science + craft. Over the years I picked my studies to learn to come up with the most tractable solutions by leveraging both the artistry, and the technical optimizations in my research.

Valve's Employee Handbook discusses the need for high breadth developers with deeper expertise in certain fields. In addition, an interest in different parts of the spectrum is shown to bring innovation and to achieve great, dare I say it, "synergy".
