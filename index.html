Welcome

I'm Tudor and I enjoy developing interactive escapism.

I have degrees in Computer Science, Industrial Design, and Game Technology, a programmer with a feel for creativity.

I work with and research Gameplay, Procedural Content, Virtual Reality, Tools, VFX, and am a GPGPU enthusiast. Data Oriented Design, OOP.

Let's make cool things together! I aim to let art and gameplay shape and be shaped by the tech; synergy between (emerging) affordances. And I know about the Dunning–Kruger effect.

I agree with the Valve Handbook advice for devs to be interested in a couple of different parts of the spectrum. It helps bring about tractable solutions, optimization, and good team mojo.
