Welcome.

I'm Tudor and I enjoy developing interactive escapism.
Let's see if we can be best buds.

I have degrees in Computer Science, Industrial Design, and Game Technology.
I'm an aficionado of gameplay, simulations, data oriented programming, and like researching PCG, VFX, and physics or mechanics using GPGPU.

I'm a design oriented programmer working on tech.
I deconstruct the layers behind the state of the art, strive to come up with the most tractable solutions, and research ways of leveraging emerging affordances.

Valve's handbook discusses the need for devs with deep expertise as well as a high breadth of skills, and that an interest in different parts of the spectrum brings all sorts of totally rad things.
